Code to read data from Benewake TF02-Pro lidar module using USB-UART device. With the device looking across a road at a height of about 50 cm, it gets a fairly consistent distance reading from most cars as they pass by, but the received signal strength varies a lot from sample to sample as the car passes by, due to glints from different angles on the body panels. Due to the size of the beam it averages over an area of maybe 50 cm diameter at a distance of 10 meters.
